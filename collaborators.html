About

Collaborators

Wikimedia Research is part of a broader community of scholars and students of Wikimedia projects and other open collaboration platforms and communities. Collaboration with researchers inside and outside of the Wikimedia Foundation – from academia, industry, and the movement – is what keeps our research environment vibrant.

Collaborations have included researchers from institutions such as: EPFL, Cornell University, Google Jigsaw, Stanford University, and the University of Minnesota. You can view a full list of our current collaborators on our formal collaborations page.

All formal collaborations are subject to the Wikimedia Foundation's Open Access policy – a policy ensuring that all output resulting from a collaboration (code, data, publications) be made available and released in the open without restrictions, to the benefit of our volunteer community and other researchers.

If you are interested in learning more about our formal collaboration process, please read these frequently asked questions, or contact us.
